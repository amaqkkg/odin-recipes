Lasagna


A fresh lasagna from the oven

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to your liking.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
